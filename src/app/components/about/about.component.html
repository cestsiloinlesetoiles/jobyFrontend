About Joby

Connecting talent with opportunity since 2020

Our Story

Joby was founded in 2020 by a team of industry professionals who recognized a gap in the job market. Traditional job boards weren't meeting the needs of modern professionals or employers seeking specialized talent.

What started as a small startup with just 5 employees has grown into a platform serving thousands of job seekers and companies worldwide. Our innovative approach to job matching has revolutionized how people find meaningful careers.
